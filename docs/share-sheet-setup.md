# Put Grain in your Share sheet

Grain lives behind the Share button — no new app to open, no account.

## iPhone (2-minute Shortcut)

1. Open the **Shortcuts** app → **+** → name it **Grain**.
2. Tap **ⓘ** → turn on **Show in Share Sheet**. Set **Accept** to **URLs**
   and **Text**.
3. Add **Get URLs from Input** (Shortcut Input).
4. Add a **Text** action containing (with the **URLs** variable right after
   `u=`):

   ```
   https://grain-keithmichaelepk-9963s-projects.vercel.app/v?u=[URLs]
   ```

5. Add **Open URLs** with that Text.

Done. On any video: **Share → Grain**.

The in-app version of this guide (with the URL pre-filled for you) is at
[/shortcut](https://grain-keithmichaelepk-9963s-projects.vercel.app/shortcut).

## Android (even easier)

1. Open [Grain](https://grain-keithmichaelepk-9963s-projects.vercel.app) in
   Chrome → menu **⋮** → **Add to Home screen** → **Install**.
2. That's it — **Grain now appears in your native share sheet.** Share any
   video from TikTok, X or YouTube and pick Grain.

## Instagram note

Instagram doesn't always hand a URL to the share sheet. Most reliable path:
**Copy link** inside Instagram, then paste it at the site.
